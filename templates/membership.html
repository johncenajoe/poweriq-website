The Credit System

Your Standing Is Earned, Not Given.

Every member inside Power IQ operates under a credit-based ranking system. Credits are earned through contribution, participation, and consistent conduct — and lost through inactivity or poor behaviour.

Your credit balance determines your class: from A at the top to D at the bottom. This isn't cosmetic. Your class reflects your actual standing in the community — and other members can see it.

The credit system exists because we believe standards matter. When everyone is accountable to a visible rank, the quality of the environment stays high.
